About

My name is Christopher and I'm 15 years old. I've been mowing lawns for a couple of years now. My parents provided me with the initial equipment and gas to help me get started. Ever since then I've been paying for my own gas and other business expenses.

I moved to Highpointe with my family in the summer of 2014. Within a month I started making some money with my business. The reason that I started College Bound Lawn Care is to save up for a car when I get older. After I get enough money to buy a car I will start saving money for college.
